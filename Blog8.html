Entry 8

We have completed the creation of the MFC, which serves as the settings menu for our project. With the MFC, users can easily customize their experience with touchless controls, including the option to choose between hand or nose gestures. The MFC also enables users to modify other settings, such as facial gestures, to further personalize their experience.


One of the key improvements we have implemented is the ability to select the desired CAD program from a dropdown list. The MFC will automatically update the cad.json file, ensuring that MotionInput is set to the correct mode for launch.

In addition, we have added a new "Edit Speech Phrases" button, which opens up a new dialogue. This

dialogue features a custom ListControl that can be easily edited to update the speech phrases. Any changes made in this dialogue will be automatically saved as the new speech phrases.

Overall, we are pleased with the functionality and ease of use provided by the MFC. It enables users to fully customize their experience, making it more accessible and tailored to their needs.
